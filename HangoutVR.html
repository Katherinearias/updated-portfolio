Hangout VR

A smart & immersive virtual reality application designed to allow the user to hangout with friends and family.

Hangout VR is a virtual reality application that will allow friends and family to stay in contact during times when they can’t be together. This connectivity can happen through a number of ways. Those ways will consist of (but not be limited to) virtual concerts, games, movies, trips, and nights out.

Overview



Design Question:

How can we design an app in Virtual Reality that can motivate users to communicate and engage with friends and family in a virtual world?


Solution

A virtual reality application that will allow friends and family to stay in contact during times when they can’t be together. This connectivity can happen through a number of ways. Those ways will consist of (but not be limited to) virtual concerts, games, movies, trips, and nights out.

My Contribution

I came up with the initial idea for Hangout VR, and pitched it to other group members. I encouraged my team to brainstorm ideas for different interactions in the experience, and led those brainstorming sessions. I created the initial survey to gather information on users experience with VR. I prototyped all the interactions on mobile and iterated on the design with my teammates.
