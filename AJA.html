Overview



Objective:

Transform the Atlanta Jugglers Association website to provide more to users who are not only looking to join the association, but also who are interested in learning how to juggle, hiring their services, or donating to their cause.


Solution

The navigation on the current site is cluttered and a bit confusing, as to what’s most important for our users to know. We simplified it by creating a primary navigation bar that would be most useful for users to find key features, such as how to be a part of the organization, events, about us, and how to contact.

Summary

The Atlanta Jugglers Association was in need of a website redesign. As we tested the initial website, many things that could be upgraded were pointed out. We reached out to the organization but unfortunately could not interview anyone there. We set out to make the page look more professional, but keep it fun. Our goal was to transform the Atlanta Jugglers Association website to provide more to users who are not only looking to join the association, but also who are interested in learning how to juggle, hiring their services, or donating to their cause. We felt it was valuable to help this non-profit because the Atlanta Jugglers Association does more than put on a performance. They are active members within the community aspiring to bring joy and happiness to those around them - anywhere from local parades to children’s hospital events.
